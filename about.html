About Me

Hello, I'm Luk Pui Ching (Cherry), a 19-year-old student at UOWCHK. I have a deep passion for the arts—especially drawing, theatre, and photography.

In 2023, my team won the Outstanding Collaboration Award at the Inter-school Drama Festival, an experience that taught me the true value of teamwork and the magic of theatre. I further pursued my passion by joining a drama workshop at The Little Guy Space and graduated from the 7th Youth Ensemble of Siu Yin Drama Club. I also performed in the stage play Annual Rings, which helped sharpen my acting skills.

To deepen my professional knowledge, I enrolled in the Applied Learning course "Starting from Theatre: The Art and Craft of Drama" (2023–2024).

I believe this programme will provide me with the professional training I need to enhance my performance skills and creative thinking, laying a solid foundation for my future development in theatre. I look forward to learning and growing on this platform as I strive toward my artistic journey.
